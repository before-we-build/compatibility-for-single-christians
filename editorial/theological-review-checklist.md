# Theological Review Checklist

Before this book is given to pastors, mentors, youth, or single Christians as a finished resource, check:

- [ ] Every normative claim has a clear biblical basis or is marked as pastoral application / practical advice.
- [ ] No passage is stretched into a modern compatibility rule beyond its context.
- [ ] Typology is never presented as biblical truth, spiritual authority, or a marriage guarantee.
- [ ] Single Christians are not treated as incomplete, defective, or waiting-room Christians.
- [ ] Marriage is honored as a good gift without being made an idol.
- [ ] Love, holiness, character, and faithfulness are clearly higher than compatibility.
- [ ] Church counsel is presented as service and wisdom, not control.
- [ ] Boundaries and safety are not weakened by language about patience or forgiveness.
- [ ] Ukrainian wording is natural, church-familiar, and not full of anglicisms.
