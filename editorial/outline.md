# Outline — v0.2

## Book arc

The book now follows a pastoral reader journey:

```text
single Christian's dignity in Christ
→ what the book is not: test, score, guarantee
→ Christ above compatibility
→ singleness, desire, and new family in Christ
→ why marry before whom to marry
→ relationality without pressure
→ common path and unequal yoke
→ wise company and forming influence
→ one body, many members without caste
→ character before chemistry
→ love above compatibility
→ boundaries, safety, and distance
→ tools under Scripture
→ three-level questions as map, not verdict
→ before dating: who am I becoming?
→ during dating: questions, pace, counsel
→ how church can help without control
→ compatibility has place, not throne
```
