# Thesis — v0.2

## Main thesis

Ввічливість допомагає не ранити людину в моменті. Сумісність допомагає мудро будувати зв’язки, які ведуть до миру, збудування, служіння і духовного росту. Але сумісність не є вищою за любов, характер, святість і вірність Христу.

## Short formulation

**Сумісність має місце, але не престол.**

## Reader-facing promise

This book helps single Christians and their churches think about compatibility without making it an idol, honor singleness without romanticizing loneliness, and ask better questions before serious relational decisions.

## Theological order

1. Christ above compatibility.
2. Scripture above model.
3. Love above comfort.
4. Character above chemistry.
5. Holiness above romantic intensity.
6. Church wisdom above isolated self-certainty.
7. Tools as servants, not authorities.

## What the book may say

- The Bible teaches relational wisdom, shared path, difference of gifts, peace, edification, love, holiness, and wise counsel.
- Compatibility can be a practical wisdom category when kept under Scripture and character.
- A model can help ask better questions, but cannot replace prayer, observation, counsel, repentance, or church wisdom.
- Love is wider than compatibility, but wisdom helps discern format, distance, pace, boundaries, and responsibility.

## What the book must not say

- “The Bible teaches typology.”
- “Type determines the destiny of a relationship.”
- “Compatibility guarantees marriage.”
- “Incompatible people should not love one another.”
- “Single Christians are unfinished.”
- “Difficult relationships are automatically sanctifying.”
- “Love means ignoring danger.”
